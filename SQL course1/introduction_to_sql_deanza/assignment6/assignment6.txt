Relationships

1) College(parent) can have zero or more departments(child).
2) Department(parent) may have zero ore more courses(child).
3) A course(parent) has zero or more sections(child).
4) College(parent) can have one or more locations(child).
5) Department(parent) can have one or more locations(child).
6) Zero or more sections(child) can be held at one location (parent).
7) Each user(instructor) may teach zero or more sections(child).
8) One user(parent) can have one user student registration(child).
9) One user(parent) has one resident id(child).
10) Zero or many sections(parent) may have zero or many student registrations(child).
11) One student registration(child) will have one payment id(parent).
